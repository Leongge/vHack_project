Bantuan Awal Persekolahan (BAP)

Cash assistance under the 2024 Budget provided by the Malaysian Ministry of Education (KPM) for all students with the aim of easing the burden of children's schooling expenses borne by parents/guardians in preparing children's equipment for the upcoming school session

Payment is made as a one-off (one time payment) of RM150 to each student from January 2024. For new Year 1 and Form 1 students, payment will be made in March 2024.

BAP assistance will be channeled through schools and distributed to students in cash or credit to their respective accounts
