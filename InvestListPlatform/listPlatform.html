Rakuten Trade

Rakuten Trade is an online stock trading platform and brokerage service based in Malaysia. It offers retail investors the ability to trade stocks, exchange-traded funds (ETFs), and other investment products through its online platform. Rakuten Trade provides users with access to various markets, including Bursa Malaysia (Malaysian Stock Exchange) and foreign markets.
The platform aims to provide a user-friendly and seamless trading experience, catering to both novice and experienced investors. Rakuten Trade offers features such as real-time market data, research reports, educational resources, and trading tools to assist investors in making informed investment decisions

  • Unit Trusts

Stash Away

StashAway is a digital wealth management platform known as a robo-advisor. It automates investment portfolio creation and management based on investors' risk profiles and financial objectives. Through diversification across various asset classes, including stocks, bonds, and alternative assets, StashAway aims to optimize returns while mitigating risk. The platform offers personalized investment strategies, transparent fee structures, and user-friendly interfaces accessible via desktop and mobile applications. StashAway operates under regulatory oversight, ensuring compliance and investor protection. Overall, it empowers investors of all levels to achieve long-term financial goals through efficient and hassle-free investing.
